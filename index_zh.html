关于我们

About Us

ISA荔湾科创社基于STEAM教育理念与ISA全人教育哲学而创立。

STEAM融合科学、技术、工程、艺术与数学五个领域，鼓励学生培养批判性思维、创造力与解决问题的能力。

我们的社团为学生提供一个自由探索科技创新的平台，让大家能够与志同道合的伙伴合作，将创意转化为现实。

The ISA Liwan Innovation Community is founded on the principles of STEAM education and ISA's holistic learning philosophy.

The STEAM approach integrates science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics, encouraging students to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Our community provides a platform for students to freely explore technological innovation, collaborate with like-minded peers, and turn ideas into reality.

我们的社团

Our Clubs

Maker创客社

Maker's Club

热爱动手创造的创客聚集地，专注于工程设计、数字制造和创新项目，如3D打印、激光切割和Arduino编程。

A hands-on space for inventors and creators, focusing on engineering design, digital fabrication, and innovative projects such as 3D printing, laser cutting, and Arduino programming.

翻车鱼乐队

Flop Fish Band

独特的校园乐队，融合音乐与科技，制作自制乐器，探索艺术、科学与声音的交融。

A unique student band that blends music with technology, crafting self-made instruments and experimenting with the fusion of art, science, and sound.

航模社

Model Aircraft Club

航空爱好者的家园，设计、制作和测试航模与无人机，通过实践项目学习空气动力学和控制系统。

A community for aviation enthusiasts who design, build, and test model aircrafts and drones, learning aerodynamics and control systems through hands-on projects.
