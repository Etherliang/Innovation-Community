About Our Club

航模社是ISA荔湾科创社旗下的新兴分社——汇聚对飞行充满热情的学生的家园。

在这里，你将学习航模与无人机的设计、制作与飞行测试，探索空气动力学、机械学与控制系统的迷人原理。

无需任何基础——只要你有一颗热爱飞行的心！

加入我们，一起从地面起飞，向创新的天空前进！

The Model Aircraft Club is one of the newest branches of the ISA Liwan Innovation Community — a place for students who dream of the skies.

Here, you'll learn how to design, build, and test model aircraft and drones, exploring the fascinating principles of aerodynamics, mechanics, and control systems.

No experience is required — just bring your curiosity and passion for flight!

Join us as we take our first steps (and flights!) together toward creating something extraordinary.
